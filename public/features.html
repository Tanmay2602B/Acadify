Product overview

Everything you need to run a high performing campus.

Acadify is a modular stack. Enable only the consoles you need or activate the full experience for admins, faculty, and students.

Admin

Control Center

  • Program, semester, and subject designer with drag-and-drop sequencing.
  • Bulk CSV importer with validation, preview, and confirm workflow.
  • Timetable generator that detects room collisions and faculty overlaps.
  • Advanced reporting with exportable CSV/PDF snapshots.

Faculty

Teaching Workbench

  • Resource uploads for notes, decks, recordings, and assignments.
  • Assessment composer with AI assistance for question banks.
  • Attendance capture, meeting scheduling, and link sharing.
  • Progress panels to track student engagement and submissions.

Students

Learning Hub

  • Personalized dashboard with program, semester, and attendance stats.
  • Timeline view of upcoming classes, meetings, and deadlines.
  • Smart reminders, AI mentor chat, and deep links to sessions.
  • Resource library with filters for notes, PPTs, assignments, and recordings.

AI

Intelligence Layer

  • Gemini 2.5 powered assistant for students and faculty.
  • Adaptive study plans and micro quizzes based on performance.
  • Insights on engagement hotspots and at-risk cohorts.
  • Auto summaries of meetings and class recordings.

Security and integrations

Secure by design

  • Role-based access, audit logs, and session controls.
  • Encrypted storage for resources and submissions.
  • Multi-factor and single sign-on ready.

Integrations

  • Jitsi for live meetings and attendance tracking.
  • CSV/Excel tooling for rapid data migration.
  • Webhook friendly events for ERP, LMS, or HR systems.
