About Acadify

Building the operating system for learning institutions.

Acadify started as a lightweight resource sharing tool and grew into a comprehensive learning resource management platform for universities, bootcamps, and training teams. We partner with academic leaders to consolidate student records, timetables, faculty workflows, meeting orchestration, and AI guidance into one modern workspace.

Mission

Clarity for every cohort

Give students, faculty, and admins a shared, realtime view of progress, meetings, and learning resources.

Vision

Human + AI campus

Blend structured academic process with intelligent assistance so every learner gets personalized support.

Promise

Launch in a week

From CSV imports to meeting automation, Acadify ships with starter kits that help campuses go live quickly.

Platform milestones

2021

Resource sharing prototype

Launched the first version to help faculty distribute notes and PPT decks to remote cohorts.

2023

Full LRMS launch

Added admin automation, timetable generator, AI assistant, and meeting tools to support entire academic cycles.

2025

Learning OS rollout

Delivered role-based dashboards with glass UI, analytics, and mobile friendly controls for multi-campus deployments.
